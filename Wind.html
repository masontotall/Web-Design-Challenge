Latitude vs. Wind Speed

Looking at this graph you could maybe make a claim that the highest wind speeds were recorded the further away from the equator the city was; however, I do not think that the data was conclusive enough to make any definite statements. As you can see on the graph there is no strong correlation of data. Most points seem evenly and randomly distributed and a possible limitation of the dataset could be the season in which the data was collected.
